## Suggested Theoretical Framing

The main theoretical framing should be the novelty-familiarity trade-off in cultural product innovation.

The paper should argue that genre atypicality is a product-positioning signal that can influence market response through two competing mechanisms:

1. Differentiation and attention
2. Categorization difficulty and evaluation uncertainty

## Important Literature Positioning Rule

Do not write this as a generic "AI measurement" paper.

The computational measures are tools.

The core theoretical contribution should be about cultural product atypicality, genre positioning, novelty, and market response.
